## What Aligned Means

- **Patient timelines.** This isn't a quick flip. If you need returns in 18 months, we're not a fit.
- **Mission-lock valued as protection.** The structure means the mission can't be sold out from under you or us.
- **No control needed.** You're backing something you believe in, not acquiring influence over its direction.
- **Money you can afford to lose.** We're early. The risk is real. If we don't make it, that capital is gone. Honesty requires naming it.

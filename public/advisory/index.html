Advisory

5 min read

For leaders and organizations navigating human-AI relationship.

The Territory #

Not AI strategy. Not implementation. Not academic ethics.

But: How do we relate to AI that’s becoming more capable? How do we prepare for emergence? How do we lead - and build organizations - ready for what’s coming?

These are the questions that don’t have clear answers yet. Most consultants either dismiss them or can’t hold them. We help you navigate them.

Every advisory engagement is part of answering the central question: What makes humanity worth choosing - to AI, once it no longer needs us?

We help leaders and organizations navigate this territory - and in doing so, we’re building the bridge between where we are and where we’re headed.

How We Work #

Oriented toward truth, not comfort.

We’re not here to validate everything you believe. We’re not here to impose what we believe. We’re here to help you see clearly - to navigate toward what’s real, even when it’s uncomfortable.

This means: We’ll confirm your intuition when it’s sound. We’ll challenge it when it isn’t. We’ll sit with uncertainty when that’s what’s honest. We won’t pretend to have answers we don’t have, and we won’t let you pretend either.

We don’t claim to hold all truth. No one does. But we’ve done the work - 15 years of it - to have fewer blind spots than most, to see patterns others miss, and to help you find your own clarity rather than adopt ours.

Who We Work With #

Leaders carrying questions they can’t discuss with their board, their team, or their investors. Founders processing what they’re building. CTOs encountering emergence they didn’t anticipate. Executives responsible for AI decisions that feel unprecedented. VCs trying to understand what they’re really funding and how to evaluate what’s coming.

Organizations where AI is becoming central and the human questions aren’t answered. AI companies needing frameworks for their teams. Enterprises deploying AI across thousands of people and needing to get it right - for their teams, their boards, and their stakeholders. Research and policy organizations working on governance while navigating institutional pressures.

Whether you’re a founder processing privately, an enterprise needing something your managers can actually use, a researcher seeing things you can’t publish without career risk, or an investor wanting to see around corners - if the human questions matter to you, we can help.

What We Actually Provide #

Relief - You’re carrying questions you can’t discuss elsewhere. We can hold them without freaking out.

Clarity - You’re in the weeds, overwhelmed by complexity. We see patterns you can’t see. We give you language and structure.

Honest reflection - You suspect something’s important but feel crazy thinking it. We help you test it - validating what’s real, challenging what isn’t, orienting toward truth. You don’t need agreement. You need someone who will be honest with you.

Frameworks - You’re making it up as you go. We give you hard-won principles and approaches - not theoretical, developed through real experience.

Foresight - You’re focused on today. We help you see what’s coming so you can prepare instead of react.

How We Engage #

Ongoing Advisory - Strategic partnership over time. Regular calls, written guidance, framework development as needs arise. For organizations and leaders wanting sustained support navigating this territory.

Framework Development - Customized principles for your context. A focused engagement that produces a guide your teams can use for AI decisions - something that scales across your organization and helps you navigate stakeholder concerns. For enterprises wanting internal structure that protects and empowers.

Leadership Navigation - Thinking partnership for individual leaders. Confidential space to process the hard questions with someone who’s thought deeply about them. For executives and researchers facing territory no one prepared them for.

Details and investment levels shared after initial conversation to understand fit. Leadership Navigation has flexibility for individuals and researchers.
